About search suggester

Have you ever spent too much time looking for an answer to a coding question, only to find that most people talking abouto your issue were using slightly different words? Sometimes the slightest difference in terminology can be the difference between quickly finding your answer and spending 5-10 minutes (or more!) reading through search results and thinking of new queries.

I created search suggester to solve this exact problem.

Type in a query that you might use in Stack Overflow, and Search Suggester provides 5 alternatives to consider that may improve your search results. Search Suggester finds synonym-like alternatives, combines them in numerous ways, and returns the combinations that are most probable. It also includes a Query Constructor that puts you back in the driver seat.
